NADIGAYAR THILAGAM

Genre: Biopic

Overview

Nadigayar Thilagam:Nadigaiyar Thilagam is the Tamil dubbed version of Mahanati, a biopic on the late legendary South actress Savitri. Keerthy Suresh plays the titular role in this Nag Ashwin-directed film, which also stars Dulquer Salmaan as Gemini Ganesan and Samantha as a journalist named Madhuravani.

Details

  • Release Date: May 11, 2018
  • Director: Nag Ashwin Reddy
  • Cast: Keerthy Suresh , Dulquer Salmaan , Samantha Akkineni
